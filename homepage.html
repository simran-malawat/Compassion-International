Welcome to Compassion International

Compassion International is a Christian child development organization that works to release children from poverty and enable them to become responsible, fulfilled Christian adults. We work with local churches and communities to provide children with education, health care, nutrition, and spiritual guidance.

Join us in our mission to release children from poverty in Jesus' name. Your support can make a difference in the lives of children around the world.
